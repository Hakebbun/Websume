Kevin Hugh 
Hello there! Looks like you've found your way to my website. Feel free to take a look around using the buttons to your left and learn a bit about me. I hope you enjoy your stay!

My name is Kevin Hugh, a Toronto-based developer and lover of all things tech. I'm currently finishing off my Computer Science Specialist Degree at the University of Toronto St. George Campus. I wasn't always a developer though. My university career started at York University, Glendon campus studying languages. I found particular interest in the way that a set of rules could govern complex communication between people. Eventually this manifested itself in my pivot into Computer Science.

As a developer I've mostly focused on Android, iOS, and Web, but I've had some experience playing with Grails, Back-End and Hardware. I've worked as an Android developer at TradeRev as well as a Web Development Instructor with Brainstation. I enjoy challenging puzzles with rewarding solutions.

In my spare time I'm often found immersing myself in Game Design - I find that unpacking and studying the experience of playing games to be as rewarding as participating in them. I thoroughly enjoy talking about what's worked, what hasn't, and what's worth exploring in games. My friends and I will often tweak rulesets to boardgames to test out our hypotheses to great success (and a ton of fun).

I've been very envolved with the Fine Arts. I was writer, cast, and choreographer for a local musical for four years, then was its assistant director in my fifth year. Applying my experience as a designer to the Fine Arts was a thrilling challenge.

Well, thats me in a nutshell! If you want to get in touch with me you can head over to my contact page and I'll get back to you as soon as I can. I hope you enjoy your time in my corner of the internet!
